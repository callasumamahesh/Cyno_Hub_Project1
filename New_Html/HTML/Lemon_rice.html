Making Of Lemon Rice

Requried Ingredients

1 cup basmati rice (cooked and cooled), 2 tablespoons cooking oil, 1 teaspoon mustard seeds, 1 teaspoon urad dal (split black gram), 1 teaspoon chana dal (split chickpeas), 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped, 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder, 8-10 curry leaves, 1/4 cup roasted peanuts, 1/4 cup cashews (optional), 1/2 cup grated coconut (fresh or desiccated), 1 lemon (juiced), Salt to taste, Fresh coriander leaves (cilantro) for garnish,

Steps for Making Lemon Rice

  1. Cook the Rice: Cook the basmati rice according to package instructions. Once cooked, spread the rice on a plate to cool.
  2. Tempering (Tadka): Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter. Add urad dal and chana dal. Sauté until they turn golden brown.
  3. Add Green Chilies and Curry Leaves: Add finely chopped green chilies and curry leaves. Sauté for a minute
  4. Add Peanuts and Cashews: Add roasted peanuts and cashews (if using). Stir and cook until they are lightly browned.
  5. Add Turmeric Powder: Add turmeric powder and mix well. The turmeric gives the rice its characteristic yellow color
  6. Mix in Rice: Add the cooled, cooked rice to the pan. Gently mix to combine with the tempering
  7. Add Lemon Juice: Squeeze the juice of one lemon into the rice. Adjust the quantity of lemon juice according to your taste preferences.
  8. Add Grated Coconut: Add grated coconut to the rice and mix well. The coconut adds a nice texture and flavor.
  9. Season with Salt: Season the lemon rice with salt according to your taste. Mix thoroughly.
  10. Garnish and Serve: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves. Serve the lemon rice hot as is or with a side of yogurt or a pickle.
